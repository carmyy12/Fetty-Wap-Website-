Present Time

Presently Fetty Wap has racked up an astonishing amount of money. He has an estimated net worth of over 8 million dollars. He is a prime example of rags to riches. He has recently purchased his first official house and currently lives there now. He owns a variety of cars, from sedans to super cars. He has 2 ferraris, a couple of C63 amgs, and several m class BMWs. He was recently robbed of his chain which is worth over 50,000 dollars. Although he has died down in fame and releases less songs, he will be remembered as a great rag to riches example.
