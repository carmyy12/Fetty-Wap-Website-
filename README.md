<h1 style="color:aqua;">Fetty Wap</h1>
<p style="color:red;">Fetty Wap ( Born Willie Maxwell II ) was born June 7, 1991 in Patterson New Jersey.</p>

<p style="color:yellow;">Fetty Wap was born with glaucoma in both of his eyes, thje doctors were only able to save one of them.</P>
<p style="color:yellow;">He wore a prosthetic eye up until his release of Trap Queen, and now openly shows off his missing eye.</P>

<p style="color:violet;">Fetty Wap is part of a gang called Remy Boyz which he started. They blew up when Fetty wap released his iconic song Trap queen.</p>
<p style="color:violet;">His gang was named after a champagne Remy Martin 1738. Trap queen is currently sitting at 500 million views on Youtube.</p>

<p style="color:orange;">When he dropped his song " Trap Queen " he instantly acquired a net-worth of 2 million dollars.</p>
<p style="color:orange;">A year later when he dropped his first album his net-worth jumped to 8 million.</p>
